NAME
----
mtf_mapper_gui - GUI for mtf_mapper

SYNOPSIS
--------
*mtf_mapper_gui*

DESCRIPTION
-----------
*mtf_mapper_gui* is a QT5 graphical frontend to mtf_mapper. Only a subset of
*mtf_mapper functionality* is available through the GUI.

SEE ALSO
--------
mtf_generate_rectangle, mtf_generate_test_chart, mtf_mapper.
